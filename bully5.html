Sometimes, words can hurt others as well.

Words, we use our whole life, we use it to communicate with others, words are powerful. Out of the mouth comes evil, depending on what kind of people use it, words can inspire people or destroy people.

In bullying, people hurt someone else, causing psychological and physical trauma to the victim. Bully has many types of, such as verbal bully, physical bully, and cyber bully.

Sometimes, verbal bully is worse than physical bully, abuse, sarcasm, malicious criticism, and negativity are all examples of verbal bullying.
